Welcome to Sidelines

Welcome to Sidelines Sports Bar & Grill, where we roll out daily specials, mouth-watering wings, and an unlimited supply of ice-cold beer and premium spirits. Located just steps from Salem State University, be sure to stop in to check out our new look and catch any sporting event you can imagine. With 40+ Large HD TV’s everywhere, including 5 outdoor TV’s on our heated year round patio, you won’t miss any of the action of your favorite team.

Food

Welcome

At Side Lines Bar, we’re all about capturing the vibe of your go-to local hangout, where great food meets great company. Our menu is packed with crave-worthy dishes like our famous wings, juicy burgers, and shareable appetizers—perfect for both meat lovers and vegetarians alike! With our signature sauces and bold flavors, Side Lines is quickly becoming a favorite spot in the DFW area. Beyond the food, we keep the fun going with games like pool, darts, and Golden Tee, along with wall-to-wall sports coverage on our HDTVs. So, pull up a chair, grab your favorite drink, and enjoy the ultimate game-day experience with us!
